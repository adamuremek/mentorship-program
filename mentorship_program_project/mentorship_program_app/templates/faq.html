About WINGS

Our Mission

WINGS is made to empower SVSU CSIS students with the knowledge, guidance, and support they need to thrive in the computing industry. By fostering meaningful connections between students and industry professionals, WINGS aims to cultivate a community of collaboration, growth, and success which will allow students to make informed decisions about their career paths before graduation.


Join us in our mission to uplift the next generation of computing professionals. Together, let's spread our wings and soar towards a brighter future in technology.

General Questions

How do I change my password?

  1. On the navigation bar, click the “Settings” button.
  2. Under account settings, click the change password button.
  3. In the old password box, type in your old password.
  4. In the new password box, type in your new password.
  5. Click the “Save Changes” button.

How do I edit my profile?

  1. On the navigation bar, click the “Profile” button.
  2. At the top right corner of the page, click the “Edit” button.
  3. You should now be able to edit information on your profile.
  4. Once you are done, click the “Save Changes” button.

How long will my account remain active?

Your account will remain marked as inactive when you have not logged on for 180 days. One week before your account is marked as inactive, you will receive an email reminding you of this change in your account.

How can I deactivate my account?

  1. On the navigation bar, click the “Settings” button.
  2. Click the “Deactivate Account” button.

Mentor Questions

How do I request a mentee?

  1. On the navigation bar, click the “Dashboard” button.
  2. Locate the desired mentee.
  3. Click the “Request Mentee” button.

How do I change my mentee limit?

  1. On the navigation bar, click the “Mentorship Group” button.
  2. At the top right corner of the page, click the “Edit” button.
  3. In the mentee limit section, click the dropdown menu and select your desired mentee limit.
  4. Once you are done, click the “Save Changes” button.

How do I view a mentee’s profile?

  1. On the navigation bar, click the “Dashboard” button.
  2. Locate the desired mentee.
  3. Click on any area of the mentee’s card to expand their profile.
  4. Once their profile is expanded, you can learn more about them.

What’s the difference between a public and a private note?

The difference between public and private notes is who can view them. A public note can be viewed by anyone in the mentorship group (students and mentor). A private note can only be viewed by the mentor in charge of the group.

How do I delete a note from the group page?

  1. On the navigation bar, click the “Mentorship Group” button.
  2. On the bottom of the page, locate your notes section.
  3. Click the “Delete” button of the note that you would like to delete.
  4. Click the “Yes” button to confirm that you would like to delete the note.

How do I remove a mentee from my group?

  1. On the navigation bar, click the “Mentorship Group” button.
  2. On the left-hand side of the page, locate the current member's section.
  3. Click the “Remove” button to the right of the mentee that you would like to remove from your group.
  4. Click the “Yes” button confirming that you would like to remove the mentee from your group.

How many mentees can I have?

You are allowed to set the limit for how many mentees you think you can actively mentor. To change this limit on your profile, locate the FAQ called “How do I change my mentee limit?”

Student Questions

How do I request a mentor?

  1. On the navigation bar, click the “Dashboard” button.
  2. Locate the desired mentors.
  3. Click the “Request Mentor” button.

How do I view a mentor’s profile?

  1. On the navigation bar, click the “Dashboard” button.
  2. Locate the desired mentor.
  3. Click on any area of the mentor’s card to expand their profile.
  4. Once their profile is expanded, you can learn more about them.

What will happen when I graduate?

Once you graduate, you will be removed from the list of available mentees. If you decide you would like to join WINGS again as a mentor, you can create a mentor account.

How do I remove myself from a mentorship?

  1. On the navigation bar, click the “Mentorship Group” button.
  2. Next to the group name, click the “Leave Group” button.
  3. Click the “Yes” button to confirm that you would like to leave the mentorship group.

Can I have multiple mentors?

Each mentee is only allowed to have one mentor at a time. If you would like to request a mentor, locate the “How do I request a mentor?” FAQ and if you would like to leave a mentorship, locate the “How do I remove myself from a mentorship?” FAQ.
